**Cross-cutting notes (from F/H):**
- True draft-day SPOFs: local machine, home network, single OAuth token, in-memory draft state. All four are cheap to harden (persistence + resume, hotspot, checklist) — do it in week 4, not draft week.
- Yahoo is the only irreplaceable provider (sole source of league/draft state); its rate limit is shared between bulk imports and live polling — schedule them apart.
- FantasyPros 100-calls/day cap needs an explicit daily call budget owned by the ingestion scheduler.
- The build critical path has no slack; R3 is the meta-risk that makes every other mitigation optional in practice. Phase gates are the control.
